1. **Body-Safe Materials**: Silicone, glass, stainless steel, and ABS plastic are non-porous and easy to clean.

3. **Noise Level**: If discretion is important, check for "whisper-quiet" models.  
4. **Waterproofing**: Allows for easy cleaning and use in the shower or bath.  
5. **Adjustable Settings**: Multiple vibration patterns or intensities help tailor the experience.
