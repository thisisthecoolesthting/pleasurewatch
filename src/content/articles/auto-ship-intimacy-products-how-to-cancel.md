## What Are Auto Ship Intimacy Products?

Auto ship (auto-ship, autoship) programs provide customers with scheduled deliveries of personal care or pleasure products—like lubricants, condoms, massage oils, or toys—without needing to reorder each time. These subscription services aim to combine discretion, convenience, and cost savings, often offering a small discount for signing up.

From my experience analyzing sexual wellness retail, brands leverage auto ship to maintain consistent supply of essentials that customers might otherwise forget to reorder, such as daily-use lubricants or supplements that support intimacy. However, this convenience comes with the responsibility to track and, if necessary, terminate shipments when preferences or needs change.

## How Auto Ship Cancellation Typically Works

### 1. Identify Your Subscription Terms

Before attempting cancellation, locate the original purchase confirmation or subscription email detailing the terms. Key info includes:  
- Billing interval (monthly, quarterly, etc.)  
- Minimum subscription length  
- Cancellation window (e.g., cancel 5 days before next shipment)  
- Contact instructions for cancellation

### 2. Log in to Your Online Account

Most reputable intimacy product retailers provide a user portal where you can:  
- Manage shipping frequency  
- Update payment or delivery info  
- Cancel or pause shipments

Look for a “Subscriptions,” “Auto Ship,” or “Manage Orders” tab after logging in. In my hands-on tests, many cancellations are processed instantly through these self-serve dashboards.

### 3. Contact Customer Service If Needed

If online cancellation isn’t available or if you face issues, reaching out to customer support becomes necessary. This usually occurs by:  
- Email form or direct email address  
- Phone number (keep note of call time and rep name)  
- Live chat support on website

Always save your cancellation confirmation number or email reply. Experienced shoppers report this minimizes the risk of accidental charges post-cancellation.

## Tips to Avoid Unexpected Charges

- Set calendar reminders for billing or shipment cutoff dates.

- Don't rely solely on unsubscribing from marketing emails—subscriptions often are separate systems.  
- Verify your bank or card statement after cancellation to confirm no new charges.  
- Reach out to customer support immediately if billed post-cancellation.

## FAQ

### How do I cancel an intimacy product auto ship subscription?

Start by logging into your account on the vendor’s website to find the subscription management page. If that option is unavailable, contact customer support by email or phone, providing your order and account details.

### Can I cancel an auto ship subscription anytime?

Most brands allow cancellation anytime but may require advance notice before the next billing or shipment. Some have minimum subscription commitments, so early cancellation might not be eligible for a refund.

### Will I be charged if I cancel late?

If you miss the cancellation deadline (typically a few days before the next shipment), you may still be charged and receive that month's package. In some cases, you can contact support to discuss exceptions.

### How can I confirm my subscription is canceled?

Always request and save a cancellation confirmation number or email. Check your online account to ensure the subscription status is “canceled” or inactive. Monitor billing statements afterward.

### Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?

Some brands offer pause or skip shipment options, which can be good if you want a break but may continue the subscription afterward. This usually retains your customer benefits without full cancellation.
